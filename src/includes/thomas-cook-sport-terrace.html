Best seats in the house!

You can enjoy the best seats in the house by booking yourself into the Thomas Cook Sport Terrace at one of our home Vitality Blast fixtures this summer.

This unique and informal hospitality area is exclusive to Thomas Cook Sport customers. Situated at mid-wicket on the east side of the ground, the Terrace has a ‘village green’ feel to it and offers a unique vantage point, right on the boundary rope. The Terrace opens 90 minutes before start of play and stays open for approximately 30 minutes after stumps with a cash bar.

What’s Included?

As well as access to the Terrace which offers comfortable sofa seating, picnic tables and parasols, you will receive a complimentary welcome drink, a gourmet picnic box, access to an exclusive cash bar and a seat in The Thomas Cook Terrace Grandstand. During the game you are free to come and go from the Terrace as you please.
